Our Beginning

A Story Born From Love of Island

Anura Tours was founded by Anura Perera, a native of Kandy who spent his childhood exploring the island's every corner — from the highland tea estates to the ancient ruins of Polonnaruwa.

After years working with international tour companies, Anura saw an opportunity to offer something different: deeply personal, authentically local, and genuinely unforgettable experiences that no mass-market operator could replicate.

Today, our team of 8 passionate guides and coordinators collectively speaks 6 languages and has personally visited every site on our tour map — ensuring we only recommend what we genuinely love.
